About Me

I’m a software specialist based in Copenhagen, currently working at the City of Copenhagen.

I’ve spent the past 8 years building digital solutions across several areas. I started out as an RPA developer, where I received two awards for my work. From there, I helped establish the municipality’s voice and chatbot team, followed by a short period working with low-code initiatives as a Technical System Owner, responsible for the technical aspects.

Today, I’m part of a team developing our internal AI solution for the City of Copenhagen, based on OpenAI. I’m primarily responsible for the technical frontend, with a strong focus on creating intuitive and user-friendly experiences, mainly using Angular.

I hold a Professional Bachelor’s degree in Software Development from KEA (2017).

Originally from Argir in the Faroe Islands, I grew up playing handball and football. I now live in Copenhagen, Denmark.
